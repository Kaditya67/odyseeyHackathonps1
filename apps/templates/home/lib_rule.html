Library Rules and Regulations
  • 1. All library users must present their library card upon entry.
  • 2. Silence must be maintained in all library areas.
  • 3. Food and beverages are not allowed inside the library.
  • 4. Users are responsible for any materials borrowed and must return them on time.
  • 5. Personal items should be stored in designated areas; bags are not allowed in the reading areas.
  • 6. Users must respect the privacy of others and refrain from disruptive behavior.
  • 7. Report any damages or issues with library materials to staff immediately.
  • 8. Violations of library rules may result in the suspension of library privileges.
